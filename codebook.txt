Codebook: Predicting NBA Shot Outcomes Through Machine Learning
Dataset: NBA Shot Logs (2014-15 Season)
Source: Noelte, D. (2016). NBA shot logs [Data set]. Kaggle. https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/dansbecker/nba-shot-logs

Total observations: 127,835 (after cleaning)
Total variables used in modeling: 6

---------------------------------------------------------------
RESPONSE VARIABLE
---------------------------------------------------------------

outcome
  Type: Factor (2 levels)
  Values: "made" = shot was scored, "missed" = shot was not scored
  Notes: Derived from original SHOT_RESULT variable. Free throws (1 pt attempts) excluded.

---------------------------------------------------------------
PREDICTOR VARIABLES
---------------------------------------------------------------

shot_dist
  Type: Numeric (continuous)
  Unit: Feet
  Description: Distance between the shooter and the rim at the time of the shot.
  Original column: SHOT_DIST
  Notes: Shots beyond 35 feet taken in the final 2 seconds of a quarter were removed as outliers (heaves).

defender
  Type: Numeric (continuous)
  Unit: Feet
  Description: Distance of the closest defender from the shooter at the time of the shot.
  Original column: CLOSE_DEF_DIST
  Notes: Higher values indicate a more open shot (less contested).

shot_clock
  Type: Numeric (continuous)
  Unit: Seconds
  Description: Time remaining on the 24-second shot clock when the shot was taken.
  Original column: SHOT_CLOCK
  Missing values: 5,346 observations (shot clock turns off when less than 24 seconds remain in a quarter).
  Handling: Missing values imputed with median during preprocessing.

handle_time
  Type: Numeric (continuous)
  Unit: Seconds
  Description: Total time the shooter possessed the ball before taking the shot.
  Original column: TOUCH_TIME
  Notes: Retained over dribble due to higher predictive value; captures both dribbling and stationary possession time.

quarter
  Type: Factor
  Values: "1", "2", "3", "4", "OT" (overtime, originally 5+)
  Description: The quarter in which the shot was taken.
  Original column: PERIOD
  Notes: Any period >= 5 was recoded as "OT". Dummy encoded during preprocessing.

---------------------------------------------------------------
VARIABLES CONSIDERED BUT EXCLUDED
---------------------------------------------------------------

dribble
  Reason: Correlation of 0.92 with handle_time. handle_time was retained as the more informative predictor.

pts_type
  Reason: Correlation of 0.73 with shot_dist. Redundant given shot_dist is already included.
